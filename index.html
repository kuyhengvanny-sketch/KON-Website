Why we started KON

We were high school friends
who asked a simple question

It started with a question we asked ourselves in high school — "how can we, as the next generation, actually do something for Cambodia?" We weren't sure what the answer was. So we started looking.

We researched problems online. We talked to people. And we kept coming back to the same issue — maternal health in Cambodia. Pregnant women, especially outside the big cities, often have no one to turn to when they have a question. They don't know what's normal. They don't know when to worry.

"We went to Kampong Cham — where I grew up — and did a real survey in the local community. What we found was difficult to see. Mothers had limited access to maternal health knowledge. Simple questions went unanswered."

— Vanny, Founder

That's when KON became real for us. Not as a business idea — but as something we genuinely wanted to exist. A place where a pregnant woman anywhere in Cambodia can talk to a real doctor and get a real answer. No travel. No waiting. No judgment.

What KON does

Clarify your doubts,
anytime

KON is an online consultation platform that connects pregnant women with verified doctors and midwives — so you can get answers to your questions without visiting a hospital.

You book a slot. You pay online. You join a video or voice call. Your provider has already read your notes before joining, so every minute is focused on you.

Every provider on KON is verified through our 3-step process — license check, ID check, and a personal interview. We do not list anyone we have not spoken to ourselves.

💬
Ask anything
No question is too small. Nutrition, symptoms, birth preparation — your doctor is here to listen.
✅
Only verified providers
Every doctor and midwife holds a valid Ministry of Health license. Verified by us personally.
🏠
From your home
Video or voice call. No travel, no waiting room. Just you and your doctor.
💜
$10 per consultation
Affordable for every family. Good information should not be a privilege.
